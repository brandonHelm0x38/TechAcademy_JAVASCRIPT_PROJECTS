To realize that any Decentralized approach requires some level of central coordination, is a massive thing to realize and part of the reason Strategic Pivot aimed to do everything so differently. Education Market Goals & Opportunities

Our goal in the education market is to leverage interactive concept mapping to enhance learning outcomes by providing students and educators with tools to visualize and understand complex relationships between concepts. Opportunities include integrating this technology into digital textbooks, online courses, and classroom activities to foster deeper comprehension and engagement.

As stated above, Strategic Pivot is aimed at creating diverse competition in the market-place by enabling different social media platforms and educational content providers to develop and offer the best interactive quiz tools for educators, thereby driving innovation and improving learning outcomes.

We believe our approach is key to transforming not just education, but as education organizes for this initiative, it will prove to other industries the power of this general model, and in truth, it will uncover a potential plan for the National Debt Crisis.

Mental Health Market Goals & Opportunities

Our goal in the mental health market is to utilize interactive concept mapping to support mental health professionals in visualizing and understanding the relationships between various mental health factors, treatments, and patient progress.

Our approach dials in on a specific journaling eco-system design and related set of data-structures that can be supported by AI features for journal suggestions and relationship mapping modes. Opportunities include incorporating this technology into therapy sessions, mental health apps, and research studies to improve patient outcomes and facilitate data-driven decision-making.

Flowcharting Market Goals & Opportunities

Our goal in the flowcharting market is to provide users with intuitive tools for creating and managing flowcharts that represent complex processes and systems. Opportunities include integrating this technology into code analysis tools, business process management software, educational tools, and collaborative platforms to enhance productivity and understanding of connections between concepts.
